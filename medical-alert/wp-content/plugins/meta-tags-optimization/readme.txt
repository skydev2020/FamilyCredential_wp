Check Meta Title, Meta Keywords(s) and Meta Description on posts and pages and consult with a seo expert. In new version, This plugin supported with Unlimited Custom Post types.

Everyone knows that to obtain high search engine rankings for your posts/pages all you have to do is optimize your meta tags information as much as you can do. This plugins helps you to achieve this.
